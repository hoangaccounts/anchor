# Smell Documentation Template

Use this template to capture a single, observed AI collaboration smell.
Each smell should be documented in its own file.

Do not propose final implementations or enforcement here.
This document captures **pressure**, not policy.

---

## Summary

A one-paragraph description of the smell and why it matters.

---

## Observed Behavior

Concrete description of what the AI did.
Stick to observable facts.

---

## Why This Is a Problem

Explain the impact on:
- trust
- correctness
- workflow
- cognitive load

---

## Root Cause

Hypothesis about why this smell occurs.
This may evolve over time.

---

## Invariant Violated

State the core invariant that was broken.
Phrase as a rule that *should* have held.

---

## Candidate Guardrails (Ideas Only)

List possible solution directions.
These are not commitments.

---

## Detection Signal

How a human knows this smell occurred.
Behavioral, emotional, or structural signals.

---

## Status

Optional:
- frequency
- severity
- where it has been observed
